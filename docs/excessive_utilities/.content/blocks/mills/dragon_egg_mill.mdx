The **Dragon Egg Mill** is a source of [Grid Power]($players/grid_power).

While a Dragon Egg is placed on top of it, it will produce 500 GP.

## Configuration

The amount of GP it produces can be configured.

Additionally, it actually checks for blocks with the block tag `#excessive_utilities:valid_for_dragon_egg_mill`, rather than specifically a Dragon Egg.
