The **Angel Ring** gives the player creative flight, as well as wings on your back.

While in use, it will occupy 64 [Grid Power]($players/grid_power).

There are 6 cosmetic variants: Invisible, Feather, Bat, Butterfly, Demon, and Gold.

## Configuration

The following things can be configured
- GP cost
- Flight acceleration and max speed
- Charge amount and recharge time
