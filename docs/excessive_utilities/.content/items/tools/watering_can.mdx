The **Watering Can** can be used to quickly grow crops in an area. It works by forcing the blocks' random ticks, which allows it to work on blocks like Saplings, Crops, Grass, or any other that does things on the random tick.

Right-click Water to fill the Can, and hold right-click on a block to water the area.

If the Watering Can is used by a Fake Player, it will be **broken** and will no longer work. This is to prevent automation, use the [Reinforced Watering Can](@excessive_utilities:reinforced_watering_can) instead.

Crafting the broken Watering Can by itself will repair it.

## Configuration

The amount of Water that it drains per tick is configurable, and can be set to 0 to make it free.

The radius it waters and the speed that it forces ticks can also be configured.

Additionally, breaking in Fake Players can also be disabled.
