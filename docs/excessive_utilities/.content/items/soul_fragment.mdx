**Soul Fragments** can be obtained by right-clicking an [Etheric Sword](@excessive_utilities:etheric_sword). Doing so will **permanently** lower your max health by 1 heart. This is called [Soul Debt]($players/soul_debt).

A Soul Fragment's primary use is to be used in crafting the [Advanced Watering Can](@excessive_utilities:advanced_watering_can).

Using a Soul Fragment will increase your max health by 1 heart, allowing you to recoup your loss.
